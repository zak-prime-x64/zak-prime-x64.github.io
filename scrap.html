Required Documentation

To finalize the purchase and transfer of ownership, please have the following documents ready for our pickup agent:

  • **Vehicle Title/Registration:** Must be clear and in your name.
  • **Owner ID Proof:** e.g., AADHAAR Card, Passport, or Driver's License.
  • **Trade License (If commercial vehicle):** Applicable for fleet or business-owned vehicles.
  • **Current Keys:** All available keys and remotes.

A Bill of Sale will be provided by our agent upon final payment.
